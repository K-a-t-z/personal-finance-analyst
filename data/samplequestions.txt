Sample Test Questions:

I. Monthly totals

1.  What was my total expense in June 2025?
2.  What was my total income/settlements in June 2025 (negative amounts)?
3.  What was my net (income + expenses) in June 2025?
4.  How many transactions did I have in June 2025?
5.  What was my average expense transaction in June 2025?
6.  What were my top 3 highest expenses in June 2025?
7.  What were my top 3 income/settlement entries in June 2025?
8.  What day in June 2025 had the highest total spending?

II. Category analysis

9.  How much did I spend on Food in June 2025?
10. How much did I spend on Travel in June 2025?
11. How much did I spend on Essentials in June 2025?
12. How much did I spend on Personal in June 2025?
13. How much did I spend on Home in June 2025?
14. How much did I spend on Others in June 2025?
15. Which category did I spend the most on in June 2025, and how much?
16. Give me a category breakdown for June 2025 (totals + % share).

III. Merchant analysis

17. How much did I spend at Uber in June 2025?
18. What are my top 5 merchants by spending in June 2025?
19. For my top merchant in June 2025, how many transactions did I have and what's the total?
20. Which merchant had the largest single expense in June 2025?
21. Show all transactions where "Where?" = Uber in June 2025 (evidence rows).
22. Which merchant's spending increased the most from June 2025 to August 2025?

IV. Source analysis

23. How much did I spend using Cash in June 2025?
24. How much did I spend via Chase in June 2025?
25. Which Source accounts for the highest spending in June 2025?
26. Give a source breakdown for June 2025.

V. Comparison + edge cases

27. Compare Food spending in Apr 2025 vs May 2025. (If Apr exists; otherwise refuse)
28. Compare total spending in the first half vs second half of June 2025.
29. How much did I spend last month? (Should ask for clarification or define “last month” relative to dataset – better: ask)
30. What was my rent in Aug 2025? (If rent isn’t explicitly identifiable, should refuse or ask how to identify rent)
